THE SPECTRA OF POLYNOMIALS IN FREE (SEMI)CIRCULAR

AKIHIRO MIYAGAWA
Abstract. We show that any L2-bounded rational function in free semicir-
cular random variables is a bounded operator, which implies the coincidence
of the usual spectrum and L2-spectrum for rational functions. Based on this
observation, we also compute the spectra of several polynomials in free circular
random variables.

In Random Matrix theory, the Brown measure of a non-normal operator has an
important role. For example, it is known that the empirical eigenvalue distribution

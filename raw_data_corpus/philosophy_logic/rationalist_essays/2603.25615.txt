Abstract. We consider multifractal Mandelbrot cascades supported on pla-
nar C2 curves with nonvanishing curvature and show that their Fourier dimen-
sion is as large as possible, i.e., equal to the infimum of the lower pointwise
dimension of the measure.

Fourier dimension of Gaussian multiplicative chaos (GMC) measures. Their re-

the Fourier dimension of more general random measures, including GMC defined
